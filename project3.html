Customer Behavior Analysis

Understanding consumer preferences and optimizing marketing strategies using survey data analysis. (Data randomized for confidentiality)

Project Summary

Who Is This Project For?
This project was conducted for Razavi Perfume, a perfume producer seeking to enhance online sales and marketing strategies through customer insights.

What Are the Goals?
- Identify key customer demographics and purchasing behavior.
- Analyze factors influencing purchase decisions, including brand awareness and product preferences.
- Measure customer satisfaction using the Net Promoter Score (NPS).
- Provide data-driven recommendations to improve sales and brand engagement.

What Will It Consist Of?
- Designing and conducting a structured survey.
- Analyzing responses to determine purchase barriers and strengths.
- Evaluating brand awareness, product performance, and customer loyalty.
- Creating strategic marketing recommendations.

What Tools Were Used?
- Survey Tools for Data Collection
- Python (pandas, numpy) for Data Cleaning & Analysis
- Matplotlib for Data Visualization

Dataset Description

- The designed survey consisted of 20 questions aimed at analyzing customer behavior.
- A total of 409 participants provided responses, contributing to a diverse dataset.

Analysis Steps

1️⃣ Defining the Business Questions

- Who are the primary customers of Razavi Perfume?
- What factors influence purchase decisions?
- What are the brand's key strengths and weaknesses?
- How effective is social media engagement?

2️⃣ Data Cleaning with Python

- Handled missing values and standardized qualitative responses.
- Removed inconsistencies to ensure accurate analysis.

3️⃣ Descriptive Statistical Analysis

- Analyzed demographics, purchase behavior, and NPS distribution.
- Assessed Instagram engagement and spending patterns.

4️⃣ Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

- Determined lack of brand awareness (43%) as the biggest purchase barrier.
- Identified longevity as the most important perfume feature.
- Highlighted scent as the strongest product strength and longevity as the main weakness.
- Analyzed Instagram engagement, showing higher NPS among followers.
- Studied customer spending habits, revealing most buyers spend less than 1 million Tomans per year.

Visualizing the Results and Providing Actionable Insights

- Customer Segmentation & Targeting: Majority of buyers are men (2x more than women) aged 25-45. Marketing should focus on this segment while attracting female buyers.
- Social Media Strategy: 61% of respondents did not follow Instagram. Increasing engagement via exclusive offers and interactive content can improve retention.
- Brand Awareness Campaigns: The biggest non-purchase reason was lack of familiarity. Marketing should prioritize brand visibility.
- Product Improvement Strategy: Enhancing longevity can boost satisfaction, and lower-cost variations can help retain budget-conscious customers.
- Pricing Considerations: Most buyers spend less than 1 million Tomans annually. Offering affordable product variations can increase accessibility.
